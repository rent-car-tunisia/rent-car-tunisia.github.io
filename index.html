Good to know

Before you book

How much does it cost to rent a car in Tunisia?+
Economy rentals start from about 25 DT per day with unlimited mileage. SUVs begin near 50 DT/day and Sahara-ready 4x4s from around 80 DT/day. Booking online ahead of arrival is consistently cheaper than the airport desk.
Can I pick up at the airport?+
Yes. The providers in this guide cover all four international airports: Tunis-Carthage (TUN), Djerba-Zarzis (DJE), Enfidha-Hammamet (NBE) and Monastir (MIR). Reserve before you fly and your car is ready on arrival.
Do I need an International Driving Permit?+
EU driving licences are accepted as-is. Visitors from outside the EU should carry an International Driving Permit alongside their national licence, plus a passport and a credit card for the deposit.
Can I drive from Tunis to Djerba?+
Yes — it's roughly 520 km, about six hours, via the El Kantara causeway or the Ajim ferry. Every provider here permits travel across the whole country, so a one-way north-to-south trip is no problem.
Is this guide really independent?+
Yes. Rankings are based on price, coverage and verified reviews only — no provider pays for placement. We may earn a small referral fee when you book, at no extra cost to you, and it never changes the order.
