Frequently asked

How much does it cost to rent a car in Tunisia?

Rates start from around 25 DT/day for an economy car with unlimited mileage. SUVs start near 50 DT/day and Sahara 4x4s from 80 DT/day.

Can I pick up at the airport?

Yes. The providers we rank cover all four international airports: Tunis-Carthage (TUN), Djerba-Zarzis (DJE), Enfidha-Hammamet (NBE) and Monastir (MIR).

Is RentRank free to use?

Completely free. We earn a small referral fee from providers when you book, at no extra cost to you — and it never affects our rankings.
